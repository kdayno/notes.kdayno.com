This is a **Map of Content** (MOC): a note whose job is to link out to other notes
and give them an entry point. As your garden grows, MOCs are how you keep it
navigable.

These sample notes are here to show you how things connect. Feel free to edit or
delete any of them — they live as plain Markdown files under `content/`.

## The sample notes

- [[what-is-a-digital-garden|What is a digital garden?]] — the idea behind this site
- [[growth-stages|Growth stages]] — how I label notes as they mature
- [[quartz-syntax-guide|Quartz syntax guide]] — callouts, code, math, tables, links
- [[learning-in-public|Learning in public]] — a short essay-style note

## How linking works

When you write `[[growth-stages]]`, Quartz turns it into a link and also records a
**backlink** on the target note (scroll to the bottom of any note to see "Backlinks").
Every link also becomes an edge in the **Graph View** on the right.

> [!tip] Try it
> Open the Graph View (right sidebar) and click around — every note you see here is
> connected to at least one other.
