A common digital-garden convention is to label each note with how mature it is. It
sets expectations for the reader and reminds you what to revisit.

| Stage | Emoji | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Seedling | 🌱 | A rough, early idea. Might be wrong or incomplete. |
| Budding | 🌿 | Developing — some structure, still evolving. |
| Evergreen | 🌲 | Mature and reasonably stable. Maintained over time. |

You can show a stage however you like. Two easy options:

- Put the emoji in the note's title, e.g. `title: 🌱 Some rough idea`.
- Add a tag like `#seedling` and let the [[quartz-syntax-guide|tag system]] group them.

> [!example] This note
> Consider this one **🌿 budding** — the idea is clear but I'll refine the wording.
